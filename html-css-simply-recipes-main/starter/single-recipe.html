prep time

cook time

30 min.

servings

6 serving

Tags: Chicken Rice Pasta Breakfast

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, mash the ripe banana until smooth. In another bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter or oil.

step 2

Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Gently fold in the mashed banana. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil. Pour batter onto the skillet, using about 1/4 cup per pancake.

step 3

Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides. Enjoy your homemade banana pancakes!
